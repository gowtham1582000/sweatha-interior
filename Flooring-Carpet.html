Loop Pile Carpet Roll

Rolls of loop pile carpet are a common choice for both residential and commercial settings because of their toughness, adaptability, and low maintenance requirements. They have a textured surface that is soft and long-lasting because they are constructed of looping threads that are woven into the carpet backing.

Non Oven Carpet Roll

Non-woven carpet rolls are not only aesthetically pleasing but also quite practical. They are a great choice for high-traffic areas thanks to their sturdy design, and their synthetic fibres offer good stain and moisture resistance. Because they can be cut to size and bonded down using common carpet adhesive, they are very simple to install.
