Vinyl in roll form

To fit any room size or shape, our vinyl flooring rolls are available in a number of widths and lengths. They have few seams and are simply cut to fit around obstructions or fitted in larger areas. Because of this, vinyl flooring in roll shape is a great option for people who want their floors to have a seamless appearance and feel.

Vinyl in Strip Form

Vinyl flooring in roll form is not only simple to install, but it is also quite practical. Because to its tough design, it is a perfect choice for high-traffic areas.
