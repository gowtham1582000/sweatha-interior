About Us

Established in the year 1998, We, Swethas Interior Decors, are one reputed suppliers and dealers of Interior Products and doing lot of turn key projects in and around of Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Pondicherry.

The complete array of Interior Decors and Interior Products is demanded by various companies, residential, shops, hotels, hospitals, multinational companies, software companies and factories.

Our Mission

To Provide safe, Reliable and Cost effective all types of Interior and exterior services.

Our Vision

To create a healthier and more sustainable living environment which reflection our clients personalities and lifestyles.
