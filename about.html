About Out Company

Established in the year 1998, We, Swethas Interior Decors, are one reputed suppliers and dealers of Interior Products and doing lot of turn key projects in and around of Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Pondicherry. We are a team of passionate designers who specialize in transforming your space into a beautiful and functional environment. With years of experience, we have been able to create stunning designs for residential and commercial spaces, ranging from small apartments to large offices.

Our services include flooring, false ceiling, blinds (for window), carpentry work for commercial and residential, paintings, partition work, glass works, electrical, plumbing, civil work, ss hand rail, fibre glass wool, audio & visual solutions, Acp work, office chairs, sun control film, air curtain, PVC strip curtain, windows wallpapers. Our team consists of experts in interior and exterior design who bring creativity and innovation to every project.

We pride ourselves on our ability to work closely with our clients to understand their vision and turn their dreams into a reality. Whether you are looking to renovate your home or revamp your office, we have the skills and expertise to deliver high-quality results.
