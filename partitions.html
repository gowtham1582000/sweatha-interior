Wooden Partitions

For internal walls and dividers in buildings, wooden partitions are a classic option since they provide any area warmth, character, and a hint of natural beauty. They can be built to complement the design and aesthetic of your building out of a range of woods, including pine, oak, mahogany, and cherry.

Aluminium Partitions

Aluminum dividers are quite useful because they are portable, strong, and low maintenance. They are a safe and sensible option for any building since they can be configured to offer sound insulation, privacy, and even fire protection.

PVC Partitions

Calcium Silicate Board Partitons

Since it is waterproof, lightweight, has a wide dimension, and is simple to install, calcium silicate board is now frequently used for wall facades. It is also the solution for sloping and bending opaque wall surfaces.

Glass Partitions

Many interior designers are endorsing glass as a material because of its slick appearance, simple installation, and even simpler maintenance. Instead of employing the traditional partition walls in contemporary designs, these properties of glass enable designers to easily construct minimalist spaces while exploring and enriching them in novel ways.

Bison Board Partitions

In addition to its high fire resistance and acoustic insulation capabilities, bison has a natural resistance to weather, fungus, termite attack, and vermin infestation, making it a perfect choice for partitions in residential, commercial, industrial complexes, and public structures.

Aerocon Partitions

A ready-made EPS Cement Sandwich Panel called Aerocon Panels or Aerocon Blocks is used to build internal and external partition walls.
