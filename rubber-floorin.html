Professional Rubber Flooring Services for Your Home or Office

Rubber Flooring

Typically, rubber flooring comes in mats, tiles, and rolls, each with special qualities and advantages. Rubber tiles are common for most facilities, however depending on the size and configuration of your area, mats or rolls can be more suitable for your needs. It is advisable to consult a specialist, such as Centaur Products, to find the best option for your requirements and financial situation.

You must take the product's thickness into account in addition to the type of rubber flooring. In most circumstances, rubber that is thicker will cost more than rubber that is thin. It is frequently worthwhile to make the investment because this higher cost also comes with improved durability, a longer useful life, better underfoot comfort, and increased shock absorption.

A lot of rubber flooring options may appear to be similar at first glance, but depending on the surface options you select, they may feel different. Options for surfaces include texture and finish. Rives, dimples, studs, or diamond-plate patterns are examples of textures that have an impact on the finished product's appearance and feel.

You can choose the ideal rubber flooring items for your design because they can be made to resemble wood, stone, and other materials in both appearance and feel. Also, some products come in bespoke colours, allowing you to choose the ideal solution to match your present colour scheme.

Above all, it's critical to think about the tasks that will be carried out on your rubber flooring. For instance, a thick rubber flooring product with light texturing rather than a thin and smooth product will likely be more advantageous if you need rubber flooring for a high-traffic gym with heavy lifting equipment. It is best to consult an expert to make sure you are receiving the finest solution for your needs and budget.
